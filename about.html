About Us

After serving in World War II, Al Grover's love for the water drew him back to his Long Island, to the waterfront community of Freeport. In 1950, he acquired the property on Woodcleft Ave. in Freeport, laying the foundation for a family business that would span generations. Al Grover established a reputation as a trusted dealer and provider of high quality service, gaining the respect and support of boaters across the region. Today, Dante Grover leads Al Grover's Marine with the support of his wife, Josephine and daughter, Bridget. Strong's at Grover's, a new boat sales partnership with longtime family-owned boating resource Strong's Marine, was forged in 2016, further solidifying the Grover's legacy in the boating industry.
