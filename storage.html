High & Dry Storage

Al Grover’s Marina offers indoor and outdoor winter storage for your vessel.

Our expansive Marina is home for hundreds of boats for the winter. Indoor Winter Storage is safe, secure and offers the most protection. Space is limited and size restrictions apply. Outdoor winter storage is also available – Al Grover’s stores over 150 boats on blocks and performs winterization, shrink wrapping, off-season service/repowers and comprehensive spring commission.

For your convenience, Al Grover's Marine provides full indoor high & dry storage with valet service.

Home of Long Island's largest indoor storage facility, Al Grover's Marina can house boats up to 40' in length, providing the most convenient storage option for your boating season.

  • Fully Enclosed Rack System
  • Storage for boats up to 40'
  • Capacity for 140 Boats
  • Access 24/7 - Just give us 30 min notice!
  • Eliminates the need for bottom paint
  • Offers protection from storms, vandalism, and the elements
  • Ability to leave personal items on board
  • Increases resale valuesss
Valet Service/High & Dry

A worry free boating day awaits with Al Grover's Marine Valet and High & Dry. Your boat is stored in a secure indoor berth. Simply call to request a launch and it is retrieved within 30 minutes. Upon your arrival, your boat will be in a slip, ready for launch; it will be returned safely inside after you return. If you need your boat ready after hours, no problem: just contact us and request "overnight" and your boat will be launched at the end of the day, allowing you access that evening or early the next morning.

Store your boat with Al Grover's Marine, contact us for more information.
